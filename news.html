CFC Enters into Long-Term Strategic Partnership with Shohin Airlines

March 16, 2026 · CFC Announcement
CFC has signed a five-year strategic investment agreement with Shohin Airlines, supporting fleet expansion and aviation infrastructure development.

CFC s.r.o., osoba rizikového kapitálu has entered into a long-term strategic partnership with Shohin Airlines under a five-year investment framework.

With a planned investment program exceeding USD 200 million, the partnership aims to support fleet expansion, enhance operational capabilities, and strengthen aviation infrastructure in the region.

This transaction reflects CFC’s broader investment strategy focused on infrastructure and technology-driven sectors in high-growth markets.

We believe that civil aviation in Tajikistan offers meaningful long-term potential, and we are pleased to support Shohin Airlines in this next stage of development.

CFC values the constructive engagement of the Shohin Airlines team and looks forward to building a strong and sustainable long-term cooperation.
